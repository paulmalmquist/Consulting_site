## Healthcare (admin ops)
**Workflows**
- Scheduling and rescheduling rules.
- Prior auth and eligibility checks.
- Billing exceptions and follow-up.

**Metrics (examples)**
- Time from intake to resolved billing exception.
- % of claims needing manual rework.
- Approval cycle time for prior auth.

## Legal ops
**Workflows**
- Intake and triage for new matters.
- Document review routing and exceptions.
- Matter status updates and approvals.

**Metrics (examples)**
- Time to route a new intake request.
- % of matters missing required context.
- Escalation rate for exceptions.

## Construction / PDS
**Workflows**
- Change order approvals and thresholds.
- Bid review and compliance checks.
- RFI routing and escalation.

**Metrics (examples)**
- Cycle time for change orders.
- % of bids needing rework.
- Aging of RFIs by status.

## What stays consistent
- Clarify decision rights.
- Compress handoffs.
- Enforce governance with evidence before cutover.
