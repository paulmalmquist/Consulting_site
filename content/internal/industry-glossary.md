# Industry Glossary (Internal)

## Shared Control Terms
- **Canonical lifecycle state**: The single approved status model used across systems and reports.
- **Owner assignment matrix**: Named role-to-state accountability map.
- **Taxonomy normalization**: Standardized coding for denial reasons, write-offs, change categories, or variance classes.
- **Audit log**: Time-stamped record of state changes, approvals, and escalations.
- **Version-controlled artifact**: A controlled document/model with tracked revisions and approver context.

## Healthcare
- **837 claim**: EDI transaction used for healthcare claim submission.
- **835 ERA**: Electronic remittance advice transaction used for payment and adjustment communication.
- **CARC/RARC**: Claim adjustment and remark codes used in denial/payment messaging.
- **Denial root cause taxonomy**: Standard category model for denial analysis and owner assignment.
- **A/R > 90 days**: Receivables aging threshold used to isolate long-tail collections risk.
- **PHI escalation logging**: Recorded evidence for sensitive health information exceptions and approvals.

## Legal
- **Matter intake**: Formal workflow to register and scope new legal work.
- **Conflict check registry**: System of record for conflict search outcomes and decisions.
- **Engagement letter execution**: Approval and acceptance workflow for legal terms.
- **Realization rate**: Billed or collected value as a percentage of worked value.
- **IOLTA reconciliation**: Trust account reconciliation process requiring defensible three-way tie-out.
- **Partner write-off tracking**: Governance trail for billing reductions and rationale.

## Real Estate Private Equity
- **IC memo**: Investment committee decision package for deal approval.
- **Underwriting model version control**: Controlled revisions of return and risk assumptions.
- **Capital call lifecycle**: End-to-end process from notice issuance to funding confirmation.
- **Waterfall promote calculation**: Distribution model for sponsor/promote economics.
- **NAV reconciliation**: Tie-out process between portfolio valuation and accounting records.
- **NOI bridge**: Variance bridge from operational income drivers to reported NOI.

## Finance
- **Journal entry approval workflow**: Controlled prepare-review-approve-post sequence.
- **Sub-ledger rollforward**: Period movement reconciliation from opening to closing balances.
- **Intercompany elimination**: Consolidation process to remove internal transaction effects.
- **SOX control documentation**: Evidence set for control design and operating effectiveness.
- **Close calendar enforcement**: Controlled timing and dependency management for period close.
- **Variance bridge analysis**: Reproducible decomposition of period-over-period financial movement.

## Construction
- **RFI lifecycle**: Request for Information process from issue through response and closure.
- **Submittal registry**: Controlled list of required submittals and review status.
- **Change order approval chain**: Structured approval path for scope/cost/schedule changes.
- **SOV reconciliation**: Schedule of Values tie-out for progress billing and earned value.
- **Cost-to-complete validation**: Forecast discipline for projected final cost (EAC).
- **Pay application audit trail**: Evidence chain for billing package approvals.

## Project & Development Services (PDS)
- **Development budget versioning**: Controlled revision history for development budgets.
- **GMP validation controls**: Checks ensuring guaranteed maximum price assumptions are governed.
- **Headcount-to-fee allocation**: Attribution model linking labor plans to commercial fee structure.
- **Milestone gating framework**: Governance checkpoints before advancing project phases.
- **Contractor performance scoring**: Standardized rubric for vendor/delivery quality assessment.
- **Client reporting package standardization**: Reproducible portfolio reporting structure and definitions.
